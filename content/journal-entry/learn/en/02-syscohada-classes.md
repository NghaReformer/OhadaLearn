# The 9 SYSCOHADA Account Classes

## Overview

The SYSCOHADA chart of accounts (**Plan Comptable OHADA**) organizes all accounts into **9 classes**. The first digit of any account number tells you its class.

## Balance Sheet Classes (1–5)

| Class | French Name | English Name | Examples |
|-------|------------|--------------|----------|
| **1** | Comptes de ressources durables | Equity & Long-Term Liabilities | `101` Capital social, `162` Emprunts bancaires |
| **2** | Comptes d'actif immobilisé | Fixed Assets | `241` Matériel, `2841` Amort. matériel |
| **3** | Comptes de stocks | Inventory | `31` Matières premières, `37` Marchandises |
| **4** | Comptes de tiers | Third-Party Accounts | `401` Fournisseurs, `411` Clients, `4431` TVA |
| **5** | Comptes de trésorerie | Cash & Bank | `521` Banque, `57` Caisse |

## Income Statement Classes (6–7)

| Class | French Name | English Name | Examples |
|-------|------------|--------------|----------|
| **6** | Comptes de charges | Expenses | `601` Achats marchandises, `661` Salaires |
| **7** | Comptes de produits | Revenue | `701` Ventes marchandises, `706` Services |

## Special Classes (8–9)

| Class | French Name | English Name | Examples |
|-------|------------|--------------|----------|
| **8** | Comptes des autres charges/produits | Exceptional Items (HAO) | `831` Charges HAO, `841` Produits HAO |
| **9** | Comptes analytiques | Cost Accounting | Used for management/cost accounting |

## The Numbering System

Account numbers follow a hierarchical logic:
- **First digit** = class (e.g., `6xx` = expense)
- **First two digits** = sub-class (e.g., `60x` = purchases)
- **Three+ digits** = specific account (e.g., `601` = purchases of merchandise)

This makes it easy to locate any account and understand its nature at a glance.
