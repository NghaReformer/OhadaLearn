# Common Journal Entry Patterns

## 1. Cash Sale

Selling merchandise for cash (no VAT for simplicity):

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| `521` Bank | 1,500,000 | |
| `701` Sales - Merchandise | | 1,500,000 |

*Asset (Bank) increases → debit. Revenue (Sales) increases → credit.*

## 2. Credit Purchase

Buying merchandise on credit:

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| `601` Purchases - Merchandise | 800,000 | |
| `401` Accounts Payable | | 800,000 |

*Expense increases → debit. Liability (Payable) increases → credit.*

## 3. Payment to Supplier

Settling an accounts payable balance:

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| `401` Accounts Payable | 800,000 | |
| `521` Bank | | 800,000 |

*Liability decreases → debit. Asset (Bank) decreases → credit.*

## 4. Depreciation Entry

Recording monthly depreciation on equipment:

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| `681` Depreciation Expense | 250,000 | |
| `2841` Accum. Depr. Equipment | | 250,000 |

*Expense increases → debit. Contra-asset increases → credit.*

## 5. Salary Expense

Recording employee salaries due:

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| `661` Salary Expense | 3,000,000 | |
| `421` Employees Payable | | 3,000,000 |

*Expense increases → debit. Liability (Payable) increases → credit.*

## 6. Owner Capital Contribution

An owner invests cash in the business:

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| `521` Bank | 10,000,000 | |
| `101` Share Capital | | 10,000,000 |

*Asset (Bank) increases → debit. Equity increases → credit.*
